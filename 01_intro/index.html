Tattoo Stencil Machine

I recently got my first tattoo! During a tattoo session, the artist usually must take their client’s inspiration images and test the trace of the image as a tattoo, which can take a long time. Before actually drawing the tattoo, an artist must usually hand draw a stencil and print out a tattoo transfer with a stencil machine. I want to build a machine that can take an inspiration image, turn it into a stencil design digitally, and print it out on tattoo transfer paper.

Uniqlo-inspired Library Scanning Bin

One task of being a Library Desk Assistant is scanning books in and out. This can be a long, manual process. My mother and I shop at Uniqlo, where you put your clothing in a bin which records the price and item for you to check out. It uses RFID tags to automatically identify what's in the bin. I would love to create something similar but for Lamont Library. I could put books in a bin and scan them all out or in at once.

Recycling Press

Every week, I take out my trash and recycle. Recycling facilities prefer that items are flattened before they are received. I want to create a low-cost, household press for recycling items or breaking down boxes. I'm am very easily by hydraulic press ASMR, so something similar but for household use would be cool.

Smart Navigation Cane

Places that are busy — city streets, airport lines, college campuses, concerts — can be difficult to maneuver, and even more difficult for those with visual impairments. I want to build a cane that has navigational capabilities using haptics and audio to help guide people through large built environments.
